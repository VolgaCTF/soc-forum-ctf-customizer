Greetings everybody and welcome to SOC-Forum CTF brought to you by VolgaCTF.org team!

To take part in the competition, you should sign up with an email address, your team's name (Latin characters only) and a logo. Warning! You will not be able to change your team's name once registered so please be careful in order not to take other team's name by accident. The number of team members is not limited.

Please specify a valid email address to get a verification email. It will be impossible to change the email address after you confirm your participation by clicking the link in the verification email.

Besides, we kindly ask you to specify your country in the registration form - we are collecting this information for statistical purposes.

The contest starts on November 22 at 12:30 MSK/10:30 UTC and lasts 5 hours. Flags match /SOCCTF{[\x20-\x7F]+}/ unless stated otherwise. Check the News page at times - there might be some important pieces of information about the competition. We will answer your questions and make other announcements in Telegram public chat.

Competitors are forbidden to perform destructive actions against competition infrastructure (*.ib-bank.ru and *.volgactf.org) and to prevent other teams from playing. A team violating the rules will be disqualified.

While conversing in the Telegram public chat, you are expected to be respectful to each other and to restrain from using swear words. Exchanging flags is strictly prohibited. A person asking for, or trading their flags will be expelled from the chat.

We reserve the right to amend these regulations at any time.
